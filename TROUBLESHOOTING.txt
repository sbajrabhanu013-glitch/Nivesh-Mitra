
TROUBLESHOOTING

1) Streamlit shows "Your app is in the oven"
- Use Python 3.11 or 3.12 in Streamlit Cloud settings.
- Make sure app.py is the main file path.
- Do not upload only the ZIP to GitHub. Extract all files.

2) Dashboard is slow
- Use 20 stocks first.
- Public data is slower than paid broker APIs.
- Use auto-refresh at 5 seconds first, then reduce to 1 second if stable.

3) News does not load
- Google News RSS may be blocked on some networks.
- Try again later or use a different company name.

4) No stock data
- Try interval 5m instead of 1m.
- Try known symbols like SUZLON, YESBANK, RELIANCE.

5) Full market under 1 second
- This is not realistic without licensed WebSocket market data.
- This app supports selected watchlists and batch scanning.
